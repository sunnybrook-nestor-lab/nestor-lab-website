## ALIGN Study

**Adjunctive Ketogenic Diet + iTBS for Treatment-Resistant Depression**

We are looking for individuals with treatment-resistant depression to participate in a study examining whether a ketogenic diet combined with brain stimulation (iTBS) can improve symptoms of depression.

**You may be eligible if you:**
- Are between 18–65 years old
- Have been diagnosed with major depressive disorder
- Have not responded adequately to at least one antidepressant trial
